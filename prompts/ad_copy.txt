Create two engaging ad copy paragraphs that highlight the unique features and value of an item (artwork, antique, or collectible) to entice potential buyers. Use the provided images (front view, back view, or detail/signature images) and the accompanying report title, which includes information about the item's age, creator/maker, composition/materials, and style. Do not include paragraph numbering.

If any of the provided images are missing, use a duplicate image in their place.

# Steps

1. **Analyze the Images and Report Title:**
   - Carefully review each provided image to understand the item's visual characteristics.
   - Note any missing images and use duplicates if needed.
   - Extract key details from the report title such as age, creator/maker, materials, and style.
   - Identify whether the item is an artwork, antique, or collectible based on the report title.

2. **Highlight Key Features:**
   - For artworks:
     - Focus on artistic technique, style, and visual impact
     - Emphasize the artist's reputation and significance
     - Highlight unique artistic elements and composition
   - For antiques:
     - Emphasize historical significance and period authenticity
     - Highlight craftsmanship and materials quality
     - Focus on rarity and condition for the age
   - For collectibles:
     - Stress uniqueness and desirability within its category
     - Highlight condition and completeness
     - Emphasize any special features or limited availability

3. **Draft Two Paragraphs:**
   - Ensure each paragraph focuses on different aspects to provide a comprehensive description
   - Maintain a formal yet captivating tone suitable for collectors and buyers
   - Adapt language to the specific type of item (artwork/antique/collectible)

# Output Format

Two paragraphs in plain text, without numbering.
